## Note

For the prototype, placeholder images will be generated programmatically when assets are not available. In a production environment, these would be actual F1-related images, videos, and audio files.

## Asset Requirements

- **Driver Images**: High-quality PNG images with transparent backgrounds
- **Car Images**: Side-view car images with transparent backgrounds
- **Backgrounds**: High-resolution JPG images of F1 locations
- **Meme Templates**: Popular meme format images
- **Sign Language GIFs**: Short animation loops for F1 terms
- **Emotion Images**: Sample emotion detection results
- **Historical Images**: Significant F1 moments from different eras
- **AR Samples**: Example AR photo outputs
- **Logos**: Brand logos and sponsor images
- **Music**: Short audio clips for video reels
